You are a young, callow youth. You grew up on a farm, where not much happened. You are incredibly bored. But then, something happened. Marauding bandits attacked the farm, burning everything and killing your parents. you were away in the woods, chopping firewood, and daydreaming that something interesting would happen. Congratulations, it did.

You are now standing in front of your burning farm. You can see the bandits strolling casually away from the flames, laughing . All you have with you is your clothes, a leather waterskin, an wheelbarrow full of firewood, and an axe. What do you do?

  • Throw more wood on the fire. You never liked living on a farm anyway.
  • Try to save you parents by rushing in and putting out the fire with your waterskin.
  • Charge the bandits screaming with your axe. You'll make them pay for this.
  • Sit down and cry.
  • Stealthily follow the bandits, put all that time hunting deer with your father to good use.
